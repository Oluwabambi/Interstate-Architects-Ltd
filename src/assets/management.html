Management

Festus Adeleke Adesina

Chairman of the Board

Joined Interstate Architects Ltd, then known as Watkins Gray Woodgate, Nigeria in June 1972 as a Project Manager. He later rose to the position of Managing Director of the company from January 1987 to 2000, by 2001 he had become the Executive Chairman, of the company.

With over fifty years involvement in the building Industry, he is highly experienced in project administration and management.

Olusegun P. Adetokunbo Ladega, FNIA

Managing Director, CEO

A Chartered and Registered Architect with 40 years' consultancy practice experience in Architectural Design, Construction Contract Administration, Project Management, Facilities Management, Arbitration and Dispute resolution. His portfolio of successfully completed projects cover a diverse range of Building Types. Well known in the construction industry, Olusegun is a Past President of the Association of Consulting Architects Nigeria (ACAN), former National Treasurer Nigerian Institute of Architects (NIA) and a member the Board of Trustees, Guild of Adjudicators in Nigeria (GAIN).
